How to use

  1. Open the 🗺️ tab to toggle layers.
  2. Set your region in ⚙️ Settings for targeted alerts.
  3. Click storm polygons/lines for damage details.
  4. Use wind arrows to assess field conditions.
  5. Export buoy data for crew deployment.
