📍 Why You Should Be In All These Systems

Registering with utilities and government procurement platforms provides you with opportunities you wouldn’t otherwise see:

✅ 1. You Get Email Notices of Bids & Opportunities

Most utility portals automatically send solicitations and sourcing event notifications once you’re registered.

✅ 2. Your Company Becomes Searchable by Buyers

Procurement teams actively search vendor databases for storm/restoration work, especially after significant weather events.

✅ 3. Many Utilities Require Prequalification Before Awarding Work

You often need:

Insurance documents

Safety programs

Capabilities statements

Past performance

OSHA / Avetta compliance

before you can even bid or get called for work.

✅ 4. Many Utilities Use 3rd-Party Platforms (PowerAdvocate / Avetta / ISN)

These platforms standardize contractor prequalification — utilities look here first for contractors who are storm ready.
