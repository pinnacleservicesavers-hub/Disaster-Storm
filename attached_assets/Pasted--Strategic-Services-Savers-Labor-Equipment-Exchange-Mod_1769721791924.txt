🔥 Core Module Structure
1️⃣ Skilled Labor Listings (Individual Level) For anything 
Individuals can create a profile and list:
•	Daily Rate (Climber – $650/day example let ai give suggestions on the going rate per day but the contractor or climber put the daily rate in)
•	Trade (Tree Climber, Lineman, Roofer, Painter, CDL Driver, Equipment Operator, etc.)
•	Years of Experience
•	Certifications (CDL, OSHA 10/30, EHAP, First Aid, ISA, etc.)
•	Travel availability
•	State availability
•	Equipment owned (if any)
Upload Section:
•	Driver’s License
•	Certifications
•	W9 (if 1099)
•	EIN (if applicable)
•	Insurance (if applicable)
•	Business license (if applicable)
AI Score:
The AI agent assigns a rating based on:
•	Experience length
•	Certification level
•	Insurance status
•	Past performance reviews
•	Job completion history
•	Verification strength
Result:
Contractors can filter by:
•	“CDL Required”
•	“ISA Certified Climber”
•	“Available in Tennessee”
•	“Storm-experienced”
•	“Verified Insurance”

🛡️ Platform Protection & Liability Shield
You MUST structure this correctly.
Add:
Terms of Service
•	Platform is a marketplace only
•	We do not employ contractors
•	We do not supervise work
•	We do not guarantee performance
•	Contractors are independent entities
•	Users agree to hold harmless & indemnify platform
Required Agreement Checkbox
Before booking:
✔ I acknowledge this platform only connects parties
✔ I verify licensing and insurance independently
✔ I accept all liability
Escrow Option (Optional Feature)
•	Funds held in escrow until job completion
•	Dispute resolution protocol
•	Platform fee for mediation
Structure this as:
Strategic Services Savers is a technology facilitator, not a contracting entity.
This protects you.
