Here are the electric utilities, cooperatives (EMCs), and public power systems in Montana along with official links to procurement, vendor/supplier opportunities, or where you can pursue contractor/vendor work (storm response, vegetation/ROW clearing, emergency restoration, construction, equipment, materials supply, etc.).

Montana utilities range from major investor-owned utilities to rural cooperatives and municipal systems. Not all have online vendor portals, but I’ve linked you to the official sites where vendor/contractor opportunities are posted or procurement offices can be contacted.
