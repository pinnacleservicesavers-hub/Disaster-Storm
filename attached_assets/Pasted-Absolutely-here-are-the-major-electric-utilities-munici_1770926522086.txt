Florida has a very large utility market — including investor-owned utilities (IOUs), municipal utilities, and rural cooperatives — offering many opportunities to register as a contractor/vendor and receive bids, RFPs, or supplier alerts.

🔌 Investor-Owned Utilities (IOUs)

These are the largest utilities in Florida — you’ll want to register as a supplier with each of them because they manage large storm restoration, vegetation, construction, and service contracts.

1. Florida Power & Light (FPL) — Largest utility in Florida

🔗 Supplier/Contractor Info – Register here to be considered as a supplier:
https://www.fpl.com/northwest/about/our-company/supplier-registration.html

👉 This is the main contractor portal for FPL suppliers.

2. Duke Energy Florida

🔗 Supplier & Diversity Program Info (registration & contacts):
https://www.duke-energy.com/partner-with-us/suppliers/supplier-diversity

👉 From this page, you can find supplier registration, codes, diversity programs, and procurement contacts.

3. Tampa Electric Company (TECO)

🔗 Main corporate site (procurement info usually under “Partner With Us” / Supplier pages):
https://www.tecoenergy.com/

👉 TECO is the holding company for Tampa Electric and supplies major contracts.

4. Florida Public Utilities Company (FPU)

🔗 Main website – use this to contact procurement or vendor contacts:
https://www.fpu.com/

👉 This IOU serves smaller territories but still issues supplier/vendor opportunities.

🌲 Rural Electric Cooperatives (EMCs) & Joint Agencies

These aren’t all listed publicly with direct vendor pages, but many use standard vendor registration systems such as VendorLink or their own bidding portals. Cooperatives also often list bid portals on their own sites.

Here are some key Florida cooperatives and known bid portals:

5. Clay Electric Cooperative

🔗 Bid & Vendor Portal (through LinkSystems/VendorLink):
https://bidding.clayelectric.com/

6. SECO Energy

🔗 Bidding & Vendor Registration (LinkSystems):
https://bidding.secoenergy.com/

7. Seminole Electric Cooperative, Inc.

🔗 Bid Portal (LinkSystems):
https://bidding.seminole-electric.com/

🏙️ Municipal Electric Utilities & Their Procurement

Florida has 30+ municipal electric utilities (public power systems) offering registration opportunities. Here are key ones with procurement/vendor pages:

8. Kissimmee Utility Authority (KUA)

🔗 Procurement & Vendor Info:
https://kua.com/about-kua/corporate-information/procurement/

👉 Includes vendor setup forms, bid lists, and contacts.

9. Gainesville Regional Utilities (GRU)

🔗 Vendor & Procurement Page:
https://www.gru.com/Work-With-GRU/Procurement

10. JEA (Jacksonville Electric Authority)

🔗 Main Site (use to find vendor/procurement links or contact them for registration):
https://www.jea.com/

JEA is one of Florida’s largest public utilities and contracts widely for electrical, vegetation, and construction work.

🧠 Tips For Contractor Registration

To get visible and eligible for work:

✔ Complete each vendor portal’s profile
✔ Upload COI (insurance), W-9, safety plan, certifications
✔ Subscribe to bid notifications and alerts
✔ Reach out to procurement contacts for introductions
