One correction (important for drone ops)

Your note says you “can’t fly at night without waivers.” That used to be true. Since 2021, Part 107 pilots may fly at night without a waiver if you’ve completed the updated training/knowledge test and your drone has anti-collision lighting visible ≥3 SM. (You still need airspace authorization at night in controlled airspace.)
