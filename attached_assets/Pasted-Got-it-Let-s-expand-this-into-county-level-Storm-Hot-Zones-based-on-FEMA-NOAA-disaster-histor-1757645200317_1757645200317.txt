✅ How You Can Use This in Your App

Contractor Filters:

Contractors choose counties instead of just states.

Example: “Alert me for Chatham (GA) + Harris (GA) + Muscogee (GA).”

Risk Tiers:

High/Very High → Push notifications immediately.

Moderate → Store but don’t push unless severe warning overlaps.

Disaster History Tagging:

Each county row can have a FEMA Disaster ID history link.

Lets contractors/insurers click and see past hurricanes/tornadoes in that county.

Map Layer:

You can shade counties by risk level in a live storm-tracking view.
