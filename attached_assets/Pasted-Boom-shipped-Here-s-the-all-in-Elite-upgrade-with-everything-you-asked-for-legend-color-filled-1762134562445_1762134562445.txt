Notes & tweaks

If SendGrid isn’t set, the email endpoint returns a clear "SENDGRID_API_KEY not set" error.

Map rendering uses lon/lat bounds of hazards. For big areas, you can tune the padding or draw only intersecting hazards to keep it tight.

You can easily add carrier-specific email templates (logos, headers) in email.py.
