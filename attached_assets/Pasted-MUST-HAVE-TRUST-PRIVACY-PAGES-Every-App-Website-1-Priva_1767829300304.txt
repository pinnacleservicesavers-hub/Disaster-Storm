MUST-HAVE TRUST & PRIVACY PAGES (Every App + Website)
1) Privacy Policy (Required)

This is the #1 legal protection page and is REQUIRED by:

App Store (Apple)

Google Play Store

Most payment processors

Any platform collecting user data

What it must include:

✅ What data you collect
✅ Why you collect it
✅ How you store it (encryption, secured systems)
✅ Who you share it with (if anyone: Twilio, Stripe, Google, etc.)
✅ Cookies + tracking (if website)
✅ User rights (delete data, request copies)
✅ How users contact you about privacy
✅ Policy “Last Updated” date (important!)

2) Terms of Service / Terms & Conditions (Required)

This protects you from:

lawsuits

misunderstandings

users claiming your app “promised” something

liability from decisions made based on Evelyn

Must include:

✅ No guarantee of results (weather, predictions, money estimates, claim outcomes)
✅ Users are responsible for their decisions
✅ Liability limitation (you’re not responsible for losses due to using app)
✅ Dispute resolution / arbitration clause (optional but VERY helpful)
✅ Termination clause (you can suspend users)
✅ Acceptable use policy
✅ “Last Updated” date

3) Trusted Data & Sources Page (STRONGLY Recommended)

Since Evelyn is giving:

weather facts

storm predictions

traffic logistics

environmental data

legal education on claims laws

You MUST clearly explain:
✅ where data comes from (NOAA, NWS, FEMA, DOT, etc.)
✅ that data is sometimes delayed or inaccurate
✅ you do not control these sources
✅ the user should verify critical info (evacuation orders, etc.)
✅ “Last Updated” + “Data refreshed every ___ minutes/hours”

This page builds trust and protects you legally.

4) Disclaimer Page (EXTREMELY IMPORTANT for Evelyn)

Evelyn should have multiple disclaimers because she speaks about sensitive topics.

You need these disclaimers:
✅ Weather & Disaster Disclaimer

Predictions are probabilistic, not certain

Use official emergency alerts for evacuation

Not responsible for injury, loss, or damages

✅ Financial / Revenue Estimate Disclaimer

Estimates are projections only

No guarantee of earnings

Many factors affect revenue (labor, insurance approvals, market, etc.)

✅ Legal Disclaimer (VERY IMPORTANT)

Evelyn does not provide legal advice

Laws vary by state and change over time

Users should consult an attorney for legal counsel

✅ Claims & Insurance Disclaimer

Evelyn provides educational guidance, not insurance representation

Not a public adjuster (unless you are licensed)

Always confirm claim requirements with insurer
