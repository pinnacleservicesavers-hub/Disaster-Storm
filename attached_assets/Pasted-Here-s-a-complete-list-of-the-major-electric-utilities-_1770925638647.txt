🧠 How to Use This List as a Contractor

Registering with these utilities matters because they all:

✔ Operate independently with their own vendor/contractor registration systems
✔ Issue work for storm restoration, vegetation management, emergency services, right-of-way clearing, and infrastructure work
✔ May have emergency contractor lists that they activate when weather or maintenance needs arise

Many cooperatives and municipal utilities require you to:

• Complete a vendor registration form
• Upload insurance certificates & W-9
• Submit capability statements
• Provide safety programs and compliance documents

This registration makes your company eligible for paid work, especially during outages or storm seasons.
