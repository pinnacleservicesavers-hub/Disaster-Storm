(Procurement AI & Automated Bidding Addendum)

This section governs the use of the Procurement AI module (“Procurement Services”) within the Platform.

The Procurement AI module provides automated assistance tools, including but not limited to:

Opportunity monitoring

Bid drafting and document preparation

Pricing analysis and recommendation

Form auto-completion

Calendar scheduling and deadline reminders

Insurance certificate request drafting

Electronic submission assistance (where supported)

Bid tracking and analytics

🔐 POLICY FOR STORING PORTAL CREDENTIALS

(Security + Safe Wording)

Credential Storage & Security Policy

Optional Credential Storage
Contractors are not required to store procurement portal credentials within the Platform. Manual submission mode is available.

Encrypted Storage
If credential storage is enabled:

Credentials are encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption (AES-256 or equivalent).

Credentials are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+).

Credentials are stored in a secure credential vault environment.

Access Controls

Access is restricted to automated system processes only.

No human staff may access stored credentials unless explicitly authorized by the Contractor for troubleshooting.

Limited Use
Stored credentials are used solely for:

Draft submission support

Opportunity monitoring

Form retrieval

Document upload where authorized

Contractor Responsibility
The Contractor remains responsible for:

Maintaining valid login credentials

Updating passwords if changed

Complying with each portal’s terms of use

No Liability for Portal Breaches
The Platform is not responsible for:

Third-party portal security breaches

Account lockouts

Portal-side authentication changes

Revocation
Contractors may revoke stored credentials at any time.

Two-Factor Authentication
Contractors are strongly encouraged to enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) on all procurement portals.
