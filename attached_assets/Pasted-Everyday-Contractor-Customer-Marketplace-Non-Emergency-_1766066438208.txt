Everyday Contractor & Customer Marketplace (Non-Emergency Section)
Overview

Disaster Direct will include a separate, non-emergency marketplace section designed for everyday contractor services. This section is not for emergency response and operates independently from hurricane, tornado, wildfire, and earthquake response tools.

This marketplace will connect customers and licensed contractors using AI-powered photo and video analysis, automated estimates, scheduling, lead management, invoicing, and payments — all inside one app.

This platform will be available in all states and cities.

Customer Side (Free to Join)

Customers can join the app for free and request estimates by uploading photos and videos of the work they need done.
