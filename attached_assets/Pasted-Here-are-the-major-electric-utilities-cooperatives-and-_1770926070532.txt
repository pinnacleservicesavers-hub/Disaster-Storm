📍 Notes on CT Electric Market

Connecticut is partly deregulated for electricity supply, meaning you can choose a supplier separate from the utility that delivers the energy. Suppliers include Constellation, Direct Energy, Think Energy, and XOOM Energy (different from the utility companies above).

If you’re aiming to work on infrastructure, distribution, or grids, targeting utilities like Eversource, UI, and municipal utilities (via CMEEC) is most effective.

🧠 Tips for Procurement & Partnerships

✔ Check each utility’s “Supplier/Vendor/Bids” section for official registration opportunities.
✔ For cooperatives like CMEEC, reach out by phone or email to request supplier onboarding and procurement guidelines.
✔ For municipal utilities, contact their administrative or engineering departments directly — many handle local projects and upgrades.
