📌 Why You Should Register With These Utilities

Each of these entities:

✔ Has a vendor/contractor registration system
✔ Issues work for vegetation/ROW clearing
✔ Issues emergency or storm response contracting opportunities
✔ Requires pre-qualification to be eligible for work

If you don’t register, utilities cannot assign work to you — and they typically do not onboard new contractors during storms.

📍 Typical Contractor Registration Requirements

When you register with utilities, they may require:

✔ Certificate of Insurance (COI)
✔ W-9 / Tax ID
✔ Safety Program & OSHA documentation
✔ Capability Statement
✔ References / past performance
✔ Licenses (where applicable)
✔ Equipment list

Having these ready will dramatically speed up registration.
