What you’re describing is 100% possible — but it requires combining multiple AI systems together properly.

You want your app to:

🎥 Watch a video

🎧 Listen to engine/motor sounds

🧠 Analyze what’s happening

🔧 Estimate likely mechanical issue

💵 Estimate repair cost based on location

That’s a multimodal AI diagnostic system.

Let me break this down in simple, step-by-step business + technical terms.

🔧 STEP 1 — Video + Audio Analysis (Core AI Engine)

You need multimodal AI that can:

Analyze video frames (visual issues: smoke, leaks, shaking, dashboard lights)

Analyze audio waveform (knocking, grinding, belt squeal, misfire rhythm)

Combine both into a mechanical hypothesis

AI Models That Can Do This:

OpenAI Vision + Audio models

Google Gemini Vision

AWS Rekognition (visual only)

Custom sound classification model (for engine noise patterns)

🎥 Visual Detection Can Identify:

Excessive vibration

White/blue/black smoke

Fluid leaks

Engine movement

Dashboard warning lights

Belt wobble

Exhaust color

🎧 Audio Detection Can Identify:

Rod knock

Ticking lifters

Misfires

Timing chain rattle

Belt squeal

Wheel bearing grind

Brake squeal

To do this properly, you:

Extract audio from video

Convert audio into waveform + spectrogram

Feed both video frames + spectrogram into AI

🧠 STEP 2 — Mechanical Reasoning Layer

After AI analyzes raw input, you need a reasoning layer:

Example output:

“Engine knocking detected at idle. Frequency pattern matches rod knock probability 72%. Visible vibration confirms.”

That reasoning layer:

Matches sound pattern to known engine fault database

Weighs probabilities

Outputs top 3 likely causes

💵 STEP 3 — Localized Repair Cost Estimator

Now it gets powerful.

You integrate:

Google Places API

Yelp API

RepairPal API (if accessible)

Or scrape mechanic labor rates

You pull:

Labor rate in ZIP code

Average hours for that repair

Parts cost database

Then calculate:

Estimated Repair Cost:
Rod bearing replacement:
Labor: $125/hr x 8 hours = $1,000
Parts: $450
Estimated Total: $1,300 – $1,700
Confidence level: 68%

🏗 SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE FOR YOUR APP

Here’s what your stack would look like:

User uploads video

Backend extracts:

Frames

Audio track

Audio → Sound classifier model

Video → Vision model

Results → Mechanical reasoning engine

Location API → Pull local labor rates

Output → Diagnosis + Estimated Cost + Confidence %
