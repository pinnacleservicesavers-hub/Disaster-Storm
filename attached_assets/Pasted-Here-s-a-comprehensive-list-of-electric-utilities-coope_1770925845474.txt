📌 Why You Should Register with These Utilities

Utilities require contractor/vendor registration because:

✅ 1. You Must Be “In the System” to Work With Them

Large utilities will not pay contractors unless they are:

In their vendor database

Approved with compliance docs

Listed with insurance & tax info

If you’re not registered, you literally cannot receive work.

✅ 2. You Get Supplier Notifications

Once registered:
✔ You receive invitations to bid
✔ You get alerts for scopes of work
✔ You get notified when they need storm / vegetation contracts

✅ 3. Emergency Work Comes from Pre-Qualified Lists

During storms or wildfire season, California utilities:
✔ Activate pre-approved contractor lists
✔ Contact vendors already in the system
✔ Do not onboard new contractors during emergency response

✅ 4. You Must Submit Compliance Docs

Most utilities require upfront documentation:
✔ COIs / Insurance
✔ W-9 / Tax info
✔ Safety plans
✔ Qualifications & capabilities

This pre-qualifies you for assignments.
