🧱 1. Core Legal & Compliance Features to Add
1.1. Privacy Policy

A Privacy Policy is legally required if you collect any user data (emails, names, phone numbers, payment info, or location).
Include:

Data Collected: What info is gathered from customers and contractors.

Purpose of Use: Why it’s collected (e.g., matching, payments, estimates).

Third Parties: Mention integrations (like payment processors or AI APIs).

Data Retention: How long you keep user data.

User Rights: Options for users to delete, edit, or export data.

Contact Info: Email for privacy questions (e.g., privacy@pinnacleservicesavers.com
).

✅ Add a “Privacy Policy” page accessible from the signup screen and app footer.

1.2. Terms of Service (TOS)

Protects the business and outlines expectations for customers and contractors.
Include:

Service Disclaimer: You are a platform, not the direct provider of services.

Contractor Liability: Contractors are independent and responsible for their own work.

Payments & Refunds: How payments are processed, who holds funds, refund policy.

Prohibited Use: No fraud, harassment, or false listings.

Account Termination: Reasons accounts may be suspended or deleted.

Limitation of Liability: Protects you (the developer and company) from damages due to misuse, negligence, etc.

✅ Require users to check a box agreeing to the Terms before signup.

1.3. Emergency Services Disclaimer

Since the app connects users to services (plumbing, electrical, etc.), add a clear notice:

⚠️ “If you are experiencing a life-threatening emergency, call 911 immediately.
Pinnacle Service Savers is not an emergency response service.”

✅ Display this message:

On the homepage,

In the help/contact page,

And during service request submission (especially for urgent jobs).

⚖️ 2. Legal Regulations & Requirements
2.1. Privacy and Data Protection Laws

Depending on where users are located:

United States:

Follow CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) – right to delete, know, and opt out.

COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act) – no accounts for under 13.

International Users (optional):

GDPR (Europe): Requires explicit consent and clear data usage explanation.

Use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission.

✅ Add a cookie consent banner and user data deletion request form.

2.2. Contractor Legal Requirements

When contractors sign up:

Require agreement to Independent Contractor Terms, stating:

They are not employees.

They must carry their own insurance and licenses.

They are responsible for taxes and legal compliance.

Optionally require upload of:

Contractor license or business registration.

Proof of insurance (liability, workers comp).

✅ Add a “Contractor Agreement” e-sign step at signup.

2.3. Customer Responsibilities

Include a short agreement for customers:

They agree to provide accurate service requests.

They understand quotes are estimates, not guarantees.

They will not hold Pinnacle Service Savers liable for any disputes or damages caused by contractors.

✅ Display during sign-up or first booking.

🧾 4. Developer & App Liability Protection

Include clear language in your TOS:

“Pinnacle Service Savers provides a platform to connect users and service providers but does not employ or directly supervise contractors.”

“Pinnacle Service Savers and its developers are not responsible for any damage, injury, or loss arising from contractor services.”

“All users agree to hold Pinnacle Service Savers harmless from claims related to transactions made through the app.”

✅ Add this in Terms of Service and footer disclaimer.

Regular Policy Updates: Add date/version number to all policies.
