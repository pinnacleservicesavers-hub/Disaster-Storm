🔵 FEDERAL GOVERNMENT BIDDING SITES
1️⃣ SAM.gov

Website: https://sam.gov

✔ Primary federal contract site
✔ All federal agencies post here
✔ Replaced FedBizOpps
✔ Required registration for federal work

If you want FEMA, USDA, USACE, DOD, etc. — this is the starting point.

2️⃣ U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE)

Website: https://www.usace.army.mil

Major source for:
• Debris removal
• Emergency contracting
• Flood control
• Infrastructure

3️⃣ FEMA

Website: https://www.fema.gov

Vendor portal: https://sam.gov

FEMA does NOT bid separately — must be registered in SAM.

4️⃣ General Services Administration (GSA)

Website: https://www.gsa.gov

GSA Schedules (MAS contracts)
Federal supply schedules

5️⃣ USDA Forest Service

Website: https://www.fs.usda.gov

Vegetation & forestry contracts

6️⃣ Department of Transportation (DOT)

Website: https://www.transportation.gov

Highway ROW clearing contracts
