🧩 HIGH-LEVEL SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

Core AI Stack

Generative AI (GPT-5 or Claude) → language, summaries, explanations, rebuttals, contracts, reports.

Agentic AI Layer (LangChain, CrewAI, or OpenDevin) → orchestrates actions, triggers APIs, manages workflows, and makes autonomous decisions.

Knowledge + Data Layer → vector database (like Pinecone or Supabase) for storing and searching documents, contracts, claims, and comparables.

Automation Layer → connects APIs (weather, insurance lookup, Twilio, lienitnow, etc.).

User Layer (Frontend) → contractors, homeowners, insurers all have tailored portals.

🔁 END-TO-END WORKFLOW OVERVIEW
1. 🔐 Onboarding & Membership

Contractors sign up → choose one-time or monthly membership (Stripe or PayPal integration).

Upload:

Business license, insurance, equipment, certifications

Logos, company info, contact preferences

AI verifies documentation → rates contractor profile (higher for complete profiles).

Legal Module: Contractors can upload their own contract or use AI-generated ones.

Agentic AI checks legality by state, suggests edits, adds AOB (Assignment of Benefits) clause automatically.

Contractors can approve or reject AI edits.

2. 📄 Contracts & Compliance

AI evaluates uploaded contract:

Confirms legality (per state law)

Highlights missing clauses (AOB, indemnification, etc.)

Offers “Auto-Legalize” button → AI rewrites with compliant terms.

Every outgoing email or claim automatically references AOB.

AI tracks laws by state and alerts contractors if AOB isn’t recognized.

3. 🧾 Job Setup & Data Input

Contractor enters:

Billables, hourly rates, equipment costs, materials

Scope of work (for comparables)

AI generates two comparables:

True Apples-to-Apples Comparable – based on actual labor, time, and material.

Xactimate Comparable – based on standardized insurance estimates.

AI explains any variance between them.

Contractor and homeowner can download both as PDF reports.

4. 🌪️ Disaster Intelligence Center (Agentic AI)

AI constantly monitors:

Weather APIs (Opterrix, NOAA, AccuWeather, satellites, live cameras).

Predicts path, impact zones, potential property damage (AI modeling).

AI auto-alerts contractors in target zones via:

Push notifications

SMS or call (Twilio / RingCentral)

Email

Contractors can “opt-in” to standby mode for storms in certain areas.

Homeowners get invites with a link to register property and select contractors.

Opt-out always available to avoid spam or harassment.

5. 📸 Damage Detection & Live Feed Integration

App integrates drones, storm chasers, satellite feeds, and traffic cameras.

AI identifies:

Property damage (roof, siding, tree, flood, fire, etc.)

Exact coordinates and owner data (via public property records API).

Auto-creates property report → address, owner name, phone, email, damage description.

Homeowners and contractors can upload their own photos/videos → AI auto-sorts into correct job file.

AI analysis:

Estimates damage level, wind speed, hail size, tree mass, cost of materials.

Builds “damage story” for insurance claim narrative.

6. 💬 Customer & Contractor Matching

Contractors can view open leads (real-time storm-based).

Once a homeowner accepts a contractor → lead is locked for that job type.

Example: tree removal marked as “in progress.”

Contractors can continue to reach out for other open scopes.

AI tracks status: “Lead available,” “Work in progress,” “Job complete.”

7. 💼 Claims, Invoicing & Insurance Negotiation

Once job is complete:

Contractor inputs cost breakdown.

AI generates invoice + narrative + supporting report.

AI submits to insurance company (email, API, or fax integration).

AI retrieves claim number, adjuster info, company address automatically.

AI Rebuttal Mode

If insurance disputes price, AI cites policy language, case law, and “good faith” principles.

Negotiates automatically up to contractor’s minimum acceptable percentage.

If payment delayed:

Day 30 → polite reminder letter.

Day 60 → Demand Letter citing lien rights.

AI triggers lien process via LienItNow API and alerts contractor.

Year-end → AI alerts contractor to contact attorney before statute runs out.

8. 💰 Payments & Payouts

Homeowners or insurers can pay directly in app via secure payment gateway.

AI logs payment, updates status, and closes claim file.

Paid jobs raise contractor’s rating.

Reports are stored permanently in contractor + customer portals.

9. 📊 Data Intelligence & Predictive Insights

AI continuously learns from:

Completed jobs

Storm paths vs. claims

Xactimate vs. true comparables

Predicts future storm losses, regional cost trends, contractor performance.
