Reports

Reports are one of the app’s strongest tools because they automatically compile your photos, notes, and job info into a professional, branded PDF that you can send to insurance companies, homeowners, subcontractors, or even keep for internal records.

🔹 Types of Reports You Can Generate

Photo Reports

Clean, organized before/after photo packets.

Include annotations, notes, and descriptions under each picture.

Perfect for insurance claims or proving completed work.

Daily Logs / Progress Reports

A “day in review” snapshot of everything done on the job site.

Shows timestamped photos, notes, and who was on-site.

Great for keeping clients updated on multi-day jobs.

Estimate / Bid Support Reports

Use photos with markups to show what needs to be done.

Attach as supporting evidence for estimates or bids.

Final Job Reports

Before → During → After sequence.

Includes cleanup verification and safety compliance proof.

Excellent for closing out jobs with insurance adjusters.

🔹 What Goes Inside a Report

Your Logo & Branding (keeps your company professional).

Project Info (customer name, job address, dates).

Photos & Videos (auto timestamped + location stamped).

Notes & Captions (crew comments, hazard notes, cleanup confirmation).

Annotations (arrows, highlights, text callouts on damage areas).

User Info (which team member added the note/photo).

Automatic Organization (chronological order, or grouped however you choose).

🔹 How Reports Are Used

Insurance Claims
Send a detailed “damage packet” with before-and-after documentation → makes it harder for carriers to lowball you.

Dispute Protection
If a client or adjuster questions your invoice, you have timestamped evidence in one clean report.

Client Transparency
Homeowners love seeing professional reports instead of messy text messages with photos.

Internal Oversight
Managers or office staff can review a job without having to visit the site.

🔹 Example Flow (Storm Tree Removal)

Crew arrives → take “before” photos of tree on house/fence.

Annotate photos (“split trunk,” “structural hazard,” etc.).

As work progresses, capture step-by-step shots.

At cleanup, add “after” photos.

Open Reports → Create Report → select all job photos.

Add captions, adjust order, and brand with your logo.

Export to PDF or sharable link.

Send to homeowner + insurance adjuster with your invoice.

In less than 10 minutes, you have a professional evidence packet that protects you and speeds up payment.
