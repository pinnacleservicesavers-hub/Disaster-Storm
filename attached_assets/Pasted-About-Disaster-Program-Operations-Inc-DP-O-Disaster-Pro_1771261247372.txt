About Disaster Program & Operations, Inc. (DP&O)

Disaster Program & Operations (DP&O) is a private disaster management and consulting firm that specializes in helping public agencies, utilities, and municipalities plan for, respond to, and recover from disasters — especially with respect to federal disaster programs like FEMA’s Public Assistance (PA) program.

DP&O’s core services are designed to support federal emergency efforts and maximize federal reimbursement by ensuring proper documentation, planning, and compliance with FEMA requirements.

What DP&O Does for FEMA-Related Disaster Work
✅ Disaster Planning & Preparedness

Develops Debris Management Plans (DMP) and disaster response strategies for local governments and utilities.

Helps organizations prepare documentation and systems so they can meet FEMA’s planning and eligibility criteria before disasters strike.

✅ Emergency Response & Recovery Support

Assists with damage assessments and rapid mobilization after an incident.

Provides expertise in debris removal operations and compliance with federal guidelines.

Works on logistics, subcontracting coordination, and emergency resource deployment that align with FEMA expectations.

✅ FEMA Claims Management

Acts as an expert advisor for FEMA Public Assistance claims, including preparing, submitting, and justifying documentation necessary to receive reimbursements for disaster-related costs.

DP&O helps clients track, reconcile, and audit their disaster cost records so they are eligible for FEMA’s PA reimbursements.

✅ Technology and Documentation Solutions

Developed XACT Recovery, a software platform that:

Tracks disaster costs and assets,

Auto-populates FEMA forms,

Stores documentation in a federal audit-ready format,

Interfaces with emergency management systems like WebEOC.

How This Helps FEMA and Federal Disaster Programs

FEMA’s Public Assistance (PA) program reimburses state, local, Tribal, and nonprofit applicants for approved disaster costs — but to qualify, documentation must meet strict federal requirements.

DP&O supports this process by:

Improving pre- and post-disaster planning so eligible projects are properly documented.

Ensuring cost entries and damage assessments conform with FEMA standards.

Helping agencies maximize the amount of federal reimbursement they receive.

Reducing audit risk by keeping records audit-ready and compliant with federal rules.

Expertise and Leadership

DP&O is led by professionals with decades of experience in emergency response, FEMA policy, debris management, and federal reimbursement compliance — including experience working directly with FEMA procedures and disaster claims.
