What eXACT Recovery™ Is

According to Disaster Program & Operations (DP&O), eXACT Recovery™ is specialized disaster recovery and claims management software used to
✔ gather, track, and organize data from a disaster response
✔ document costs, labor, assets, and materials
✔ prepare and submit FEMA/project worksheets
✔ manage forms, evidence, photos, and audit documentation
✔ streamline the recovery reimbursement process
📍 All in one centralized, secure system.

It is not a data-or file-system recovery tool like O&O DiskRecovery or Recuva — it’s disaster program support software, not file recovery software.

📌 What It Does (Key Functions)
📁 Centralizes All Disaster Information

All costs, documentation, photos, notes, forms, labor reports, and equipment hours live in one place.

Replaces spreadsheets, email attachments, and manual filing.

📊 Tracks Damage, Costs & Assets

Maintains pre-disaster asset status tied to assessments.

Tracks equipment, crews, labor, materials, contractors, and their reported costs.

Helps reconcile debris, repair costs, and eligible disaster expenses.

📄 Auto-Prepares FEMA & Recovery Worksheets

Streamlines preparation and submittal of FEMA Project Worksheets (PW’s) for all categories A-G.

Attaches supporting documents (photos, reports, cost records).

Reduces duplicated entry and human error.

📥 Reduces Redundancy & Errors

Interfaces with existing systems to avoid re-entering data already stored.

Automates linking of damage, metadata, and cost information.

Helps catch data entry inconsistencies before submission.

📡 Provides Audit Support

Keeps complete, timestamped documentation that FEMA/state auditors require.

Makes review and reconciliation easier during close-out.

🧭 Tracks Communications

Records FEMA and state communications for organizational clarity.

🧠 Why This Matters in Disaster Work

In a disaster response environment, huge amounts of documentation drive reimbursement and compliance:

FEMA and State reimbursement hinges on matching costs with eligible work.

Auditors ask for documentation proving hours, equipment usage, rates, photos, and logs.

If documentation is incomplete or inconsistent, funds can be denied or clawed back.

eXACT Recovery™ organizes, standardizes, and automates that documentation so invoices and submittals are defensible and auditable.

🆚 What It Is Not

This software is not:

❌ A computer data recovery program (like tools such as Recuva, O&O DiskRecovery, PhotoRec, etc.) which recover deleted files from storage drives.
❌ A general purpose “paperwork bot” that fills out any PDF automatically.
❌ A personal record vault app like an auto-form-filler extension.

Instead, it is enterprise disaster recovery and claims management software, built specifically for public agencies and contractors to collect, organize, and submit disaster recovery documentation.

🧩 How It Fits Into the “Paperwork Problem”

This software helps solve your “paperwork time drain” issues by:

✔ Providing central storage and searchable documentation
✔ Linking costs to damage assessments
✔ Auto-generating required FEMA paperwork
✔ Preventing double entries and mistakes

But it does not automatically fill every form you own — it fills and organizes disaster recovery forms and claims relevant to FEMA/hazard reimbursement workflows.

📌 Summary

DP&O’s eXACT Recovery™ is a specialized disaster claims support & documentation system that:

Captures, stores, and structures disaster data

Tracks labor, equipment, and costs

Helps prepare FEMA/State reimbursement submissions

Makes audit support easier
Not a general data recovery or personal auto-form tool.
