📍 Why You Should Register with These Utilities

Each group above operates independently — and none of them automatically hire contractors outside their system unless you’re registered and qualified as a vendor.

Here’s why:

✅ 1. You Get Official Access to Procurement

Utilities use formal vendor portals — whether it’s APS’s sourcing system or SRP’s supplier registration.

Without registration:

You won’t be invited to bid

You won’t receive RFPs / bidding alerts

You won’t show up in their internal vendor searches

✅ 2. You Become Eligible for Emergency / Storm Work

Utilities pre-qualify contractors before storms. When outages or vegetation hazards occur:

✔ The utility contacts registered vendors first
✔ They activate pre-approved contractor lists
✔ They dispatch crews from internal rosters

If you are not on that list, you won’t get called.

✅ 3. You Reduce Risk for the Utility

Utilities require:

Insurance certificates

Safety programs

OSHA compliance

W-9 / tax info

Key personnel credentials

Larger utilities will not work with you until they’ve verified your compliance.

✅ 4. Many Projects Require Vendor Registration

Even non-storm opportunities — like vegetation management, right-of-way clearing, construction support, fleet services — require:
✔ Official vendor codes
✔ Contract number or W-9 on file
✔ Registration in their procurement systems
