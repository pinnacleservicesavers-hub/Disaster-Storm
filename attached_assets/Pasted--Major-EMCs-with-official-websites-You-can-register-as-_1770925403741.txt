📍 Why These Matter

These EMCs are utilities you can register with as a contractor if you want to:

Provide vegetation/ROW clearing

Provide storm debris removal or hauling

Support emergency restoration

Provide trucking, equipment, rentals, or labor

Provide vegetation management or right-of-way services

They are local electrical utilities, often with direct contractor/vendor portals or bidding information on their websites.
