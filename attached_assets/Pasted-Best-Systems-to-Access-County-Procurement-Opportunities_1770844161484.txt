Best Systems to Access County Procurement Opportunities

These platforms aggregate county, city, municipal, school district, and special district bids in one place — much easier than hunting down 3,000+ individual websites.

✅ 1. DemandStar – County, City & Local Government Bids

🔗 https://network.demandstar.com/

• Connects government agencies with vendors for bids at county and local levels.
• Covers thousands of agencies across the U.S.
• Vendors get notified based on commodity/keyword matches.

✅ 2. BidNet Direct – State & Local Government Bids (County Included)

🔗 https://www.bidnetdirect.com/

• Many counties post county-level RFPs through BidNet groups per state.
• State purchasing plus local/county contracting in one portal.

✅ 3. FindRFP – Public Sector & County Contracts

🔗 https://www.findrfp.com/

• Central database for state, county, city, municipal, utilities, schools, hospitals, etc.
• Covers bids across all levels of government in the U.S.

✅ 4. PublicPurchase – Nationwide Local Government Bid Notifications

🔗 https://www.publicpurchase.com/

• Free bid notification system for local governments, counties, and municipal agencies.
• Vendors can sign up for alerts by commodities/services.

✅ 5. BidPrime – County, State & Local Bid Aggregator

🔗 https://www.bidprime.com/

• Searches over 110,000 government agencies including counties for bid opportunities.

✅ 6. Euna Supplier Network (Bonfire) – Local Government & County Bids

🔗 https://supplier.eunasolutions.com/

• Formerly Bonfire — widely used by counties for RFP posting and vendor responses.
• Search county-level solicitations.
