💳 SUBSCRIPTION & BILLING TERMS

(Flat Fee Model – No Revenue Share)

1. Subscription Structure

Access to the Procurement AI module is provided solely on a flat monthly subscription basis.

The Company does not:

Charge a percentage of awarded contracts

Charge commission on bids

Charge success fees

Participate in contract proceeds

Share in revenue from awarded contracts

The subscription fee is for access to the software platform and AI tools only.

2. No Contingency or Brokerage Relationship

The Platform operates strictly as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) provider.

Nothing in this Agreement shall be interpreted as:

A brokerage agreement

A contingency fee arrangement

A procurement agent agreement

A partnership or joint venture

A profit-sharing relationship

The Contractor retains 100% of all awarded contract revenue.

3. Payment Terms

Subscription fees are billed monthly in advance.

Fees are due regardless of:

Number of bids submitted

Contracts awarded

Revenue generated

Use frequency

Failure to pay results in suspension of access.

4. No Refund Policy

Subscription fees are non-refundable.

Results (including bid awards or losses) do not affect subscription obligations.
