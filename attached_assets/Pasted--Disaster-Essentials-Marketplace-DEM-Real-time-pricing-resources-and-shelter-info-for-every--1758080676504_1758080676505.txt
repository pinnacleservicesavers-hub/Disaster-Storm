⚠️ What to Check Before Buying Satellite Devices

Coverage: Iridium = global, Inmarsat/Thuraya = regional.

Costs: Device + monthly/usage fees.

Durability: Waterproof, rugged, storm-proof.

Emergency Features: GPS, SOS buttons, push-to-talk.

Accessories: Solar chargers, extra batteries, vehicle adapters.

Regulations: Some states/countries restrict satellite phone usage.
