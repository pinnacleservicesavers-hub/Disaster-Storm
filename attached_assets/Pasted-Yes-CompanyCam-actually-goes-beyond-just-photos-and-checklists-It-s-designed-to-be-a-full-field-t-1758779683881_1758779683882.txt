Yes — CompanyCam actually goes beyond just photos and checklists. It’s designed to be a full field-to-office communication and documentation hub. Let me expand on even more things it does that I haven’t mentioned yet:

📸 Advanced Photo/Media Features

Video Capture: Not just still photos — you can take short videos to show wind movement, roof flex, or unstable limbs.

Photo Tags & Filters: Tag by type (damage, completion, estimate, hazard) so you can pull up “all hazard photos” across jobs in seconds.

Smart Groups: Automatically group photos by user, project, or date for easy review.

🗂 Project Management Add-Ons

Timeline View: Each project has a chronological “story” of everything done — photos, notes, comments, documents, and reports.

Pinned Notes: Highlight important instructions (“Use crane access only,” “Call homeowner first”) so the crew sees them right away.

Voice Notes: Record audio messages in-app — faster than typing when you’re in the field.

👷 Team Oversight Tools

User Permissions: Limit what different roles can see/do. For example, subs can upload photos but not view invoices.

User Tracking: See who took which photo and when (crew accountability).

Daily Logs: Automatically compile who was on site, what was done, and when.

📑 Reporting & Client Tools

Branded Reports: Add your logo/colors to photo reports or damage packets → looks professional to insurers & homeowners.

Progress Reports: Send homeowners daily or weekly updates without extra effort.

Customer Galleries: Share “before and after” albums easily (great for marketing).

🔌 Integrations & Workflow Expansion

CRM / Estimating Software: Connect with Jobber, Housecall Pro, Contractor Foreman, ServiceTitan, etc.

Accounting / Billing: Hook into QuickBooks Online and other accounting tools to keep invoices synced.

API Access: Advanced users can integrate directly into their own custom systems (like your storm app idea).

💸 Financial Tools (on higher tiers)

Estimates & Invoicing: Build and send estimates or invoices from the app.

Payments: Accept credit card payments directly through CompanyCam (reduces “I’ll mail a check” delays).

📱 Mobile-Friendly Extras

Offline Mode: Crews can take photos even without service → uploads when back online.

Push Notifications: Get alerts when new photos/comments are added to important projects.

Quick Capture Mode: Open straight to camera so crews don’t waste time fumbling with project setup.

🌐 Beyond the Basics

Marketing Content: Turn job photos into ready-to-post social media material.

Training Library: Use jobsite photos/videos for safety training (“what to watch for” examples).

Warranty/Guarantee Proof: Keep long-term documentation to prove work was done correctly if there’s a dispute years later.

Documenting

Communicating

Reporting

Invoicing/Payments

Marketing
