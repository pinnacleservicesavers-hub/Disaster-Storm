🔥 What Makes This One Expensive

This tree is a “technical removal” because:
✅ Power lines are tangled through the canopy
✅ Requires rigging / controlled lowering
✅ Higher chance of property damage if done wrong
✅ May require bucket truck or crane
✅ Extra crew for safety + speed
