For Contractors

The AI should give them job-ready intelligence that saves time, builds credibility with insurers, and increases revenue:

Damage Assessment & Classification

Auto-detect tree damage, roof damage, powerline strikes, flooding, etc.

Classify severity (minor, major, catastrophic).

Estimate tree height, diameter, and approximate weight.

Instant Insurance Packet Data

Generate a claims-ready summary in Xactimate-style format.

Attach annotated photos with AI-generated captions (e.g., “Oak tree, approx. 36 inches diameter, leaning on roof, structural damage visible.”).

Estimate removal time, crew size, and equipment needed (crane, bucket, etc.).

Safety & Compliance Guidance

Point out hazards (live wires, unstable trees, flood zones).

Recommend PPE or OSHA/ANSI compliance steps.

Suggest best access routes for trucks and equipment.

Job Cost & Resource Calculator

Provide rough job estimates (labor hours, equipment use, disposal costs).

Compare costs with local/state averages to stay competitive.

🏠 For Customers / Victims Watching Live or Aftermath

The AI should give them clear, reassuring, and actionable updates:

Plain-English Damage Reports

Explain what the footage shows (“A large tree has fallen across the back half of your roof, causing visible roof collapse.”).

Translate technical contractor terms into homeowner-friendly language.

Safety Alerts

Point out immediate dangers (“Avoid the backyard — downed power line detected.”).

Suggest next steps (“Do not attempt to remove debris yourself; wait for a licensed contractor.”).

Insurance & Recovery Help

Generate a simplified “claim snapshot” they can forward to their insurance adjuster.

Provide FEMA, SBA, or Red Cross links for assistance.

Live Q&A

Let victims ask: “How bad is this?” or “Will my roof need full replacement?” — and get AI-generated, easy-to-understand answers.

🔑 Bonus Features to Make It Next-Level

Before/After Comparisons – AI overlays satellite or prior drone images to show exact damage differences.

Predictive Risk Alerts – AI tells contractors if nearby trees/hazards might cause secondary damage.

Auto-Generated Work Orders – Contractors can push one button and generate a draft contract + price sheet from the footage.

Victim Portal Sync – Customers can click to request help directly from what they’re watching, and it routes to the nearest contractor.

👉 In short:

Contractors get technical, cost, and safety data to win jobs and file claims faster.

Customers get clear, calm, easy-to-understand guidance that builds trust.
