Electric Cooperatives (EMCs / Distribution Co-ops)

Nebraska has a strong cooperative system. Most cooperatives either publish bid notices on their sites or use cooperative bid systems (like VendorLink / LinkSystems). If they don’t maintain an online portal, you’ll need to contact them directly via operations / engineering / business offices.

🧠 Tips for Contractor / Vendor Registration

✔ Register your company in each vendor portal and upload COI (insurance), W-9, OSHA/safety docs.
✔ Sign up for email notifications on solicitations (RFPs/RFQs/Bids).
✔ Contact the procurement or operations departments at co-ops without online portals to get on their contractor/vendor lists.
✔ Many utilities do not post open portals — so reaching out directly with a capability statement helps you get on their internal lists.
