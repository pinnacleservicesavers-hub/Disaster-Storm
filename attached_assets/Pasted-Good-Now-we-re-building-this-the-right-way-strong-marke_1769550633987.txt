Say this:

Average time to prepare a bid reduced by X%

Average documents auto-filled per submission

Average number of bids tracked per contractor

Compliance reminder automation rate

Percentage of bids submitted on time

Number of portals monitored per contractor

Increase in bidding frequency after onboarding

Those are system metrics. Not award guarantees.
