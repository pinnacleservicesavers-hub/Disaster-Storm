3) Encoding & compression (create MP4, WebM, poster)

After exporting each raw clip from your AI tool, run these (replace INPUT.mp4 with your export). These commands produce 720p loop-friendly versions and a poster JPG.

A) Normalize length (8.0s) & downscale to 720p MP4
ffmpeg -y -i INPUT.mp4 -vf "scale=1280:-2,fps=24" -t 8 \
  -c:v libx264 -preset veryslow -crf 22 -pix_fmt yuv420p -movflags +faststart radar-loop.mp4

B) Create WebM (VP9) twin
ffmpeg -y -i radar-loop.mp4 -c:v libvpx-vp9 -b:v 0 -crf 32 -row-mt 1 -pix_fmt yuv420p radar-loop.webm

C) Extract poster JPG
ffmpeg -y -ss 0.5 -i radar-loop.mp4 -vframes 1 -q:v 3 radar-poster.jpg


If your tool can output a perfect loop, you’re done.
If not, pick the most seamless 8s segment (watch for start/end motion match). You can trim with -ss and -to.
