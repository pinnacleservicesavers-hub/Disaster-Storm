Lead Capture Components

Funnels & Websites – Build drag-and-drop funnels, landing pages, or full websites to capture leads.

Forms – Create multi-step forms, surveys, and applications that can be embedded or shared.

Calendars – Let prospects book appointments directly (integrated with Google/Outlook).

Surveys – Collect customer feedback and qualify leads with logic-based questions.

Chat Widgets – Website live chat pop-ups that capture leads and push them into pipelines.

SMS/MMS Opt-Ins – Keyword campaigns (e.g., “Text TREE to 55555”) for storm/emergency sign-ups.

Inbound Call Tracking Numbers – Track and route phone calls while capturing lead details.

🔹 Lead Nurture Components

Automated Workflows – Multi-channel nurturing via SMS, email, voicemail drops, calls, and DMs.

Email Marketing – Broadcast emails, drip campaigns, and AI-assisted copywriting.

SMS & MMS Campaigns – Personalized two-way texting and bulk campaigns.

Ringless Voicemails – Pre-recorded messages dropped into leads’ voicemail inbox.

Social DMs – Facebook Messenger, Instagram DM, and Google My Business message integration.

AI Chatbots – Automated replies for leads 24/7 until a human steps in.

🔹 CRM & Pipeline Components

Smart CRM – Centralized hub for all leads, tagged, segmented, and prioritized.

Pipelines & Stages – Drag-and-drop Kanban boards (e.g., New Lead → Estimate → Contract → Paid).

Opportunities Dashboard – See every lead, where they are in the funnel, and potential value.

Task & Reminder System – Assign tasks, set reminders, and track follow-ups.

🔹 Marketing & Advertising Components

Landing Page A/B Testing – Optimize lead conversion rates.

Google Ads & Facebook Ads Integration – Capture leads directly from ad campaigns.

Reputation Management – Automate review requests via text/email.

Social Media Posting – Schedule and automate social posts across multiple platforms.

🔹 Communication Components

Unified Inbox – Email, SMS, calls, Messenger, Instagram, and GMB messages all in one inbox.

Call Tracking & Recording – Track ROI on campaigns and record conversations for training.

Power Dialer – Rapid call campaigns for following up on new leads.

2-Way Texting on Desktop & Mobile App – Real-time engagement with prospects.

🔹 Analytics & Reporting Components

Funnel/Website Analytics – Conversion rates, drop-off points, ROI tracking.

Call & SMS Analytics – Track cost per lead and attribution.

Ad Spend ROI – Connects to Google & Facebook Ads for real cost-per-lead analysis.

Pipeline Value & Forecasting – Project revenue from current leads in the system.

🔹 Automation & AI Add-Ons

AI Lead Response – Automatically respond to new leads within seconds.

Workflow Triggers – Automations based on actions (form filled, call missed, contract signed).

Document E-Signatures – Send contracts, proposals, and agreements for signature.

Payment Links & Invoicing – Collect deposits or payments directly.
