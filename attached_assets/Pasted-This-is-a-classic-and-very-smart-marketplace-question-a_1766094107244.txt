This is a classic (and very smart) marketplace question, and the decision you make here will directly affect adoption, revenue, and trust. I’ll give you a clear recommendation first, then explain why, plus an exact structure you can implement.

Short Answer (Best Practice)

Yes, add contractors to the app, but gate lead access until they pay.

👉 Do NOT make the app empty on day one.
👉 Do NOT give free leads to unpaid contractors.

The Winning Model (Proven & Scalable)
1️⃣ Customers: 100% Free (No Friction)

Customers can:

Request services

Submit jobs

Browse categories

No payment, no login wall if possible

Why:
Customers create the demand. Demand is your leverage.

2️⃣ Contractors: Pre-Listed but “Locked”

Add contractors to your platform before they pay, but:

Unpaid contractors can:

See their profile exists

Claim their business

Edit basic info (logo, services, service area)

See lead notifications blurred/locked

Unpaid contractors CANNOT:

See customer phone numbers

Receive calls or texts

Message customers

Accept jobs

Example notification:
“New Lead Available – Upgrade to Unlock”

3️⃣ Paid Contractors: Full Access

Once they subscribe monthly, they get:

Real-time leads

Calls & texts

In-app messaging

CRM tools

Job tracking

Reviews & ranking boost

Why This Works (Psychology + Business)
🔥 1. Social Proof from Day One

When customers open the app and see:

“23 contractors available in your area”

…it feels legit.

An empty app = no trust.

💰 2. Contractors Pay Faster When They See Demand

Nothing converts like:

“You have 4 missed leads this week.”

This creates FOMO and urgency.

🚫 3. Prevents Free Riders

If contractors must pay before even being listed, many won’t.

If contractors get leads without paying, they never will.

This model avoids both problems.

Subscription Positioning (Important)

Do NOT position it as:

“Pay us to be listed.”

Instead say:

“Get exclusive, verified local leads delivered directly to you.”

Listing = free
Leads = paid
