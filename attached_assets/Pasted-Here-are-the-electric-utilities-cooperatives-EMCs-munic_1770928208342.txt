🧠 Why You Should Register with These (Contractor Perspective)

✔ Must be in their vendor systems to receive RFPs/RFQs
✔ Prequalification is often required before utility dispatches for storm or vegetation work
✔ Many EMCs need contractor COI (insurance), W-9, safety docs, equipment lists, and references in advance
✔ Most public utilities won’t onboard new vendors at time of need — must be pre-registered
